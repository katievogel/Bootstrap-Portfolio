About

Hello! My name is Katie Vogel, and I'm a developer. I am fully adept at creating highly functional websites from front to back with a particular fondness for backend and database development. I am a master of Google-Fu and take every stumped moment as an opportunity to learn.

Since 2018 I have been learning how to code through online self-study and coding bootcamps. In 2019, I completed the Full Stack Web Development coding bootcamp from Trilogy Education in partnership with the University of Pennsylvania. Determined to sharpen my skills, I picked up Python on my own and work through various coding challenges on a regular basis. Now I am studying Clojurescript, a functional lisp language that compiles to javascript for a whole different way of web development.

Currently, I work with software engineers and product teams to deliver top-tier tools for clinical trial payment processing. My experience has immersed me in the SDLC process, Agile practices, and all aspects of software development and technology. Having come from the operations and product side I a unique perspective of understanding the end user's point of view, the needs of the business, and excellent communication skills.
